About Jack's Computer Shop

Jack's Computer Shop started out as a small electronics shop in the early 1980s in Central Ohio. In 1995, we restructured to specialize in selling and repairing computers and laptops. With more than four decades of experience in electronics and three decades in repairing computers, we have expanded to five store branches across central Ohio to adequately provide reliable services for thousands of central Ohioans.
